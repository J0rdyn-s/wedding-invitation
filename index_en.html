Our Story

2007

It all started with "Hello"

We first met as penpals online, chatting endlessly late into the night. Jisu was 13, Eli was 15, and though separated by distance, our friendship quickly grew, shaping our lives in ways we couldn't yet imagine.

2014
Jan

Our First Meeting

Seven years later, Jisu flew to Taiwan to finally meet Eli in person. We spent wonderful days exploring mountains and riding bicycles through the city. Jisu left behind her diary when she returned home, but Eli made sure it safely found its way back to Korea by mail.

2022
Dec

The Night Eli Rescued Jisu

Eli visited Korea and rescued Jisu from an awkward work dinner, jokingly declaring himself her knight picking up his princess. We also traveled together to Busan, sharing laughter, good food, and unforgettable memories.

2024
Sep

From Friends to Something More

During Eli’s next visit, we explored beautiful cities like Gangneung, Yangyang, and Sokcho, and enjoyed thrilling days at Everland and Caribbean Bay. On September 1st, amidst the excitement, Eli asked Jisu, "Will you be my girlfriend?" And just like that, our long friendship blossomed into love.

2025
Feb

An Unexpected Proposal

In Paris, Eli planned a romantic proposal at Montmartre, but fate intervened when Jisu stumbled and fell on one knee, accidentally looking like she was proposing first. Thinking she’d figured out his plan, Eli quickly proposed on the spot—with a little audience of street artists cheering us on. Of course, the answer was "Yes!"

2025
Apr

Happily Ever After Begins

After years filled with handwritten letters, late-night chats, thoughtful gifts, and endless phone calls, we're finally saying “I do.” Join us as we begin the rest of our lives together, hand in hand.
